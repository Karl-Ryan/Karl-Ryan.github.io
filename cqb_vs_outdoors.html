Close Quarter Battle vs Outdoors


Airsoft can by broken down in two types.


Close quarter Battle (CQB) and Outdoors


CQB


CQB is a term that stands for Close quarter Battle.
CQB Game's Typically tack place in repurposed buildings such as old office blocks, where houses, factory's or shopping centres allowing for urban combat games / scenarios.
Meany "CQB" sites avail of this for there set game types and special themed nights such as ZOMBIE NIGHT of other similar gaming experiences.
The main advantage of a "CQB" site is that the Game is unaffected by the changing of weather allowing for Games to take place all year round.



Outdoors



Outdoor / External Airsoft is what most people imagen when first heiring of Airsoft
Outdoor Airsoft sites are typically large walled compounds with multiple staging areas.
Often these are woodland / forest like terrain allowing for greater camouflage tactics to be implemented by the participants for a Game.
. Outdoor sites lend themselves better for tournaments as they usually have a much larger occupancy capacity when compared to their CQB counterparts.

Weapon types

In Airsoft a weapon can have 4 types of power.


Spring GAS CO2 Electric

Spring


Most Pistols can be found as a Spring powered weapon, Now a days some rifles and even Shotguns are available in spring powered variants. The biggest issue with Spring weapons is that they must be re-cocked every time you pull the trigger

Go to Top

GAS

Gas powered guns often referred to as GBB guns or Gas Blow Back guns use a canister of compressed gas. Not only propel the round loaded in to the gun but to also imitate the recoil you'd receive from a real gun. GBB guns biggest down fail is that the canister of gas can run empty mid game and is not always easy to replace when taking enemy fire

Go to Top


CO2


CO2 powered guns are much like there GBB counterparts but without the Blowback, Also, with CO2 is a small canister like capsule contained in the magazine of your weapon meaning if you run out of CO2 you can just swap the Mag


Go to Top

Electric


Electric Guns are run with the use of rechargeable battery pack or cell. Most electric weapons keep their battery near the barrel or in the buttstock of the rifle. In recent years Electric pistols have entered the market allowing those interested to obtain Full / Semi-automatic pistols to complement the rifle. Down falls of electric guns is that if the battery is not cared for correctly it can corrode and will need replacing

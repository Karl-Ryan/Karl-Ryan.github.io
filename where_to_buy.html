Where to Buy

There are certain Retailers who specialise in Airsoft equipment Below are some examples of recommended retailers based in Dublin.


M.I.A.

M.I.A "Main Irish Airsoft was one of the first to offer Airsoft products in Ireland.
Today they are one of the biggest and most well-known Airsoft retailers in Ireland with a wide range of item on offer online and in store.

Hobby Airsoft

Hobby Airsoft is in Ballymount industrial estate just off the Greenhills road.
With their high accessibility and always helpful staff ready to lend a hand to all from those just looking in to Airsoft to session Airsofters in need of advice,
It's not har to see why Hobby Airsoft have so Meany repeat customers.

SG Airsoft

SG Airsoft is one of those places you normally don't know unless you or a friend have been there.
Located in Charthouse Business Centre away from prying eyes SG has a wide range of item on offer (if you can find them).
One of the best things in SG is there "Bone Yard" sails where you have the chance to buy a broken repairable, returned / unwanted item for well below market value.
The Bone Yard is a great place to find spare parts for what you already have, or a little project for yourself to try without the risk of destroying your favoured gun.
